Villas & Rooms

Standard Room

Each room comes with one queen size bed, flat screen TV, refrigerator, coffee table, with hot & cold shower.

Deluxe Room

Each room comes with two queen size bed, flat screen TV, refrigerator, coffee table, with hot & cold shower.

Family Suite

Each comes with 2-bedroom unit (2 Queen size beds& 2 pull-out bed). Each unit comes with living area, flat screen TV, refrigerator, with hot & cold shower.

Studio Type Room

Air-conditioned rooms located at second floor. Each room comes with two queen-sized beds, flat screen TV, refrigerator and a private bathroom.

Condo Type A

These condo-type units (2 BR) can accommodate up to 7 persons. These air-conditioned units are equipped with a flat screen TV, mini refrigerators, sofa bed, and a private bathroom.

Condo Type B

These condo-type units (2 BR) can accommodate up to 10 persons. These air-conditioned units are equipped with a flat screen TV, mini refrigerators, sofa beds, gas stoves, and a private bathroom.
